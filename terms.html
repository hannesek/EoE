Studio's Terms and Policies for Booking a Tattoo Session

Booking Fee

A non-refundable booking fee is required to secure an appointment. This fee will be deducted from the total cost of the tattoo on the day of the appointment.

Cancellation Policy

Clients must provide a minimum notice period for cancellations (e.g., 48 hours) to reschedule without losing their booking fee. Late cancellations may result in forfeiture of the booking fee.

Rescheduling

Clients can reschedule their appointment within a reasonable time frame without additional charges. Excessive rescheduling may require a new booking fee.

Design Changes

Significant changes to the tattoo design must be communicated well in advance of the appointment. Last-minute design changes may result in rescheduling or additional charges.

Arrival Time

Clients are expected to arrive on time for their appointment. Late arrivals may result in a shortened session or rescheduling.

Age Requirement

Clients must be of legal age to get a tattoo. Valid government-issued identification is required for verification.

Health Considerations

Clients should disclose any health conditions, allergies, or medications that may affect the tattooing process. The studio reserves the right to refuse service based on health concerns.

Under the Influence

Clients must not be under the influence of alcohol or drugs during the tattoo session. The studio reserves the right to refuse service in such cases.

Photography and Social Media

The studio may take photographs of the completed tattoo for portfolio and promotional purposes. Clients can discuss any privacy concerns with their artist.

Touch-Up Policy

The studio may offer a complimentary touch-up session within a specified period (e.g., 6 weeks) after the initial appointment. After this period, touch-ups may be subject to additional charges.

Payment

The remaining balance of the tattoo must be paid in full on the day of the appointment. Cash or specified payment methods are accepted.

Courtesy and Respect

Both clients and studio staff are expected to treat each other with respect and courtesy. Any disrespectful behavior may result in refusal of service.

Follow Aftercare Instructions

Clients must follow the provided aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing of the tattoo. Failure to do so may affect the outcome, and touch-ups may not be provided free of charge.

Legal Disclaimer

The studio is not liable for any allergic reactions, infections, or complications that may arise post-tattoo, provided proper aftercare instructions are followed.
